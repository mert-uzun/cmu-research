Property: The string contains at most two `b`’s, and it is not exactly of the form `b a^(odd) b`.

Formal description:

\[
\#_b(w) \le 2 \quad\text{and}\quad w \notin \{\, b a^{2k+1} b : k \ge 0 \,\}.
\]

Equivalently: if the string has exactly two `b`’s and starts and ends with `b`, then the number of `a`’s between them must be even.
