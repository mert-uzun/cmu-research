Property: No string in set A contains the contiguous substring `bb` followed later by the contiguous substring `aa`.

Equivalently: all occurrences of `aa`, if any, occur before all occurrences of `bb`.

Formal description: strings over `{a,b}` that are **not** of the form

`x bb y aa z`

for any strings `x, y, z ∈ {a,b}*`.
